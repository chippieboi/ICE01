About Blue Jeans Pizza

Pizza and Brew Is What We Do

Since 1980 Blue Jeans has been a Worcester tradition. Our famous Italian-style, thin crust pizza has been awarded Worcester’s Best Pizza multiple times. The roots of our recipe and hand-stretched dough go back to Boston’s North End. And the extensive menu means you’ll find something for everyone. Order online. We’d love to have you join us in our new dining room. Or have us deliver your meal hot and delicious.
